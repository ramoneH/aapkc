Our Mission

Avenue Auto Wrecking's mission is to provide an economical source of quality OEM new and used parts for lovers of European cars. We specialize in Saab, Volvo, Audi, Volkswagen, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, and Porsche. If you need a part for a car from one of these fine manufacturer's we either have it or can get it, both new and used. We also specialize in slightly damaged European rebuilders that we often have most or all of the parts needed to rebuild them.
